How to Compress Images

Our free online image compressor makes it easy to reduce the file size of your images:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust compression settings using the quality and resize sliders
  3. Choose output format (keep original or convert to JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your image
  5. Download the compressed image and save bandwidth and storage space

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading - Smaller images load quicker, improving user experience and SEO
  • Reduced bandwidth usage - Save on hosting costs and data transfer
  • Better storage efficiency - Store more images in the same space
  • Improved email deliverability - Smaller attachments are more likely to be delivered
  • Optimized social media - Compressed images upload faster to platforms
